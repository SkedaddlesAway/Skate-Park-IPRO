Skate & Create: Art Meets Skating

Event Details

Date: June 17 - June 21, 2024

This camp combines skating with creativity, offering skaters a chance to explore art through the lens of skateboarding.

Activities

  • Board decorating
  • Graffiti art workshops
  • Skate photography sessions
  • Mural painting and skate video editing

Ages: 10-16

Registration: Required. Art supplies and equipment provided.
