Thanksgiving Friendsgiving Skate Fest

Event Details

Date: November 23, 2024

Gather with friends and family for a Thanksgiving-themed skate fest! Bring your favorite dish to share and enjoy group skating, games, and gratitude activities.

Activities

  • Potluck-style Friendsgiving dinner
  • Group skate session
  • "Thankfulness Wall" for notes of gratitude
  • Skate games and prizes

All Ages Welcome: Free entry. Participants are encouraged to bring a dish to share.
