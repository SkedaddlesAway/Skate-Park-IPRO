Beginner Skateboarding Camp

Event Details

Date: June 10 - June 14, 2024

Designed for kids and teens new to skateboarding, this week-long camp teaches the basics in a safe, supportive environment.

Activities

  • Daily beginner lessons
  • Obstacle courses
  • Games on wheels
  • "Show Your Progress" showcase on the last day

Ages: 5-12

Registration: Required. Limited spots to ensure personal instruction.
