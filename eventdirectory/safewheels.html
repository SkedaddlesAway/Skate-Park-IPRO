Safe Wheels Workshop

Event Details

Date: June 19, 2024

Dedicated to skate park safety, this workshop teaches kids essential safety skills, gear use, and safe falling techniques.

Activities

  • Safety drills
  • “How to Fall Safely” practice
  • Helmet fitting sessions
  • Skills showcase

Ages: 6-12

Registration: Free for all children. Parents are encouraged to attend.
