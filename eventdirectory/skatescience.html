Skate Science and Engineering

Event Details

Date: July 15 - July 19, 2024

A unique camp that combines skateboarding with STEM learning. Skaters will learn about physics, engineering, and mechanics as they relate to skating.

Activities

  • Understanding forces and motion in skating
  • Building mini ramps and rails
  • Learning about skate park design
  • STEM experiments related to skateboarding

Ages: 10-16

Registration: Required. All materials provided.
