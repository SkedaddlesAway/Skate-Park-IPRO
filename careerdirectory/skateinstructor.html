Position Details

Position: Skate Instructor

Location: GoGo's Skate Park, [City, State]

Type: Part-time / Full-time

Salary: Hourly, based on experience

About the Role

We are seeking experienced and enthusiastic Skate Instructors to join our team at GoGo's Skate Park. As a Skate Instructor, you will provide safe and engaging instruction to skaters of all skill levels, helping them improve and enjoy skateboarding. If you’re passionate about teaching and love skateboarding, apply now!

Responsibilities

  • Provide individual and group skateboarding lessons for all skill levels and ages.
  • Assess students' skills and tailor instruction to their needs.
  • Teach basic to advanced techniques, focusing on safety and enjoyment.
  • Promote a positive and inclusive learning environment.
  • Assist in skate park events and demonstrations as needed.

Qualifications

  • Extensive skateboarding experience, with knowledge of various techniques and safety practices.
  • Previous teaching or coaching experience is a plus.
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Ability to work flexible hours, including weekends.
  • First Aid/CPR certification (preferred).

Benefits

  • Flexible scheduling
  • Competitive hourly rate
  • Free access to the skate park
  • Opportunity to work with a fun, supportive team

How to Apply

If you’re excited to share your love of skateboarding, please apply here by submitting your resume and a brief cover letter explaining your teaching experience and passion for skateboarding.
