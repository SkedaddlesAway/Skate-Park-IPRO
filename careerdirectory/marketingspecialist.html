Position: Marketing Specialist

Location: GoGo's Skate Park, [City, State]

Type: Full-time

About the Role

We are looking for a creative and dynamic Marketing Specialist to promote GoGo's Skate Park through various channels. This role will develop marketing campaigns, create content, and engage our community, both online and offline. If you’re a skilled marketer with a passion for action sports, we’d love to hear from you!

Responsibilities

  • Develop and execute marketing campaigns to increase park attendance and event participation.
  • Manage social media accounts and create engaging content for various platforms.
  • Collaborate with local businesses and sponsors for event promotions.
  • Analyze campaign performance and make data-driven adjustments.
  • Maintain brand consistency and voice across all channels.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Communications, or a related field.
  • 1-3 years of marketing experience, preferably in the sports or entertainment industry.
  • Proficiency in social media management and digital marketing tools.
  • Strong writing, communication, and creative skills.
  • Understanding of skate culture is a plus!

Benefits

  • Competitive salary and benefits
  • Flexible work environment
  • Free access to the skate park
  • Opportunities for professional growth

How to Apply

If you’re ready to take our marketing efforts to the next level, please apply here with your resume, portfolio, and a cover letter explaining your marketing experience and creative vision.
