About the Role

We are seeking a skilled Maintenance Technician to keep GoGo's Skate Park in top shape for all visitors. This role is essential in ensuring our facilities, ramps, and park equipment are safe and well-maintained. If you have a background in maintenance and a passion for skate culture, apply today!

Responsibilities

  • Perform routine inspections and repairs of ramps, rails, and skate park structures.
  • Maintain cleanliness and organization of the park facilities.
  • Assist in setup and breakdown of event equipment as needed.
  • Ensure all safety standards are met and assist in emergency repairs.
  • Maintain inventory of tools and materials.

Qualifications

  • High school diploma or GED required.
  • Previous experience in maintenance, repair, or construction.
  • Basic knowledge of tools, materials, and safety protocols.
  • Ability to lift heavy objects and work outdoors in various weather conditions.
  • Interest in skateboarding is a plus.

Benefits

  • Competitive hourly pay
  • Health benefits and paid time off
  • Free access to the skate park
  • Stable, year-round position
