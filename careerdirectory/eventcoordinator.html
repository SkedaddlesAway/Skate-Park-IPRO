Position Details

Position: Event Coordinator

Location: GoGo's Skate Park, [City, State]

Type: Full-time

Salary: Competitive, based on experience

About the Role

GoGo's Skate Park is looking for an enthusiastic and organized Event Coordinator to plan and execute memorable events that engage our community of skaters. As an Event Coordinator, you will be responsible for organizing, promoting, and managing events, from concept to completion. If you have a passion for skate culture and experience in event management, we’d love to hear from you!

Responsibilities

  • Plan and organize skate park events, including competitions, holiday-themed events, workshops, and camps.
  • Coordinate with vendors, sponsors, and park staff to ensure smooth execution of events.
  • Develop marketing materials and promote events through various channels (social media, email, etc.).
  • Manage event budgets and handle logistics, including equipment, permits, and safety protocols.
  • Engage with the skate park community to create events that appeal to skaters of all ages and skill levels.
  • Gather feedback after events and make recommendations for future improvements.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Event Management, Marketing, or a related field (preferred).
  • 1-3 years of experience in event planning, coordination, or a related role.
  • Strong organizational and multitasking skills.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Ability to work flexible hours, including some weekends and holidays.
  • Knowledge of skateboarding culture is a plus!

Benefits

  • Competitive salary and benefits package
  • Opportunities for professional growth
  • Flexible scheduling
  • Free access to the skate park

How to Apply

If you’re excited about the opportunity to bring energy and creativity to our skate park events, please apply here by submitting your resume and a cover letter explaining why you’d be a great fit for this role.
